Recently, my family and I went on holiday in Penang . We stayed in a rented apartment as our stay during our stay in Penang. We visited various interesting places that were there. Among them is that we stopped at Deen Maju Restaurant, which is one of the most famous Nasi Kandar restaurant in Penang. Next, we visited Kampung Agong where the place has a variety of interesting activities that can be done such as horseback riding, rickshaws and bicycles, strolling along the rice fields and taking pictures. After that, we stopped at Medan Selera and enjoyed a meal of grilled fish by the sea. In the evening, we had a chance to visit Street Art to see the various art painted on the walls of each street alley and take pictures as memories. After a tiring walk we rested at Padang Kota while enjoying the food sold there.
